Our Story

Boy meets girl

He was young, charming and great at crashing cars. She was stubborn, creative and excellent at eating ice cream, but in late 2005 they were on a collision course set for love. He bought her a lamp and she bought him some vests, however despite the crappy gifts they were soon shacked up together. The flat was in Canterbury; the furniture was Ikea.

Canterbury was their home for two years, they lived a fun life - James spun his decks and Lou kept painting. They bought a cat called Austin who was amazing. They started thinking to the future and they wanted their own place, plus the Ikea furniture was falling apart. Next stop Mummy Begent’s house in Whitstable.

Poor Mummy Begent put up with the couple for a year whilst they worked hard and started to save their pennies. One day (12th Feb 2009), the couple went out for an evening and James asked if Lou would be his lobster...

She said yes!

Spurring their love on, they saved a bit more and with a helping hand they bought their little house in Faversham. They said goodbye to Whitstable and took their new cat Nero and to keep him company they got him a sister Lily. They settled in and fell in love with their little town and started to plan their wedding - let's hope it all goes well.

Wedding Day

The important bit

Venue

The venue we've chosen is Mount Ephraim (pronounced 'ee-fram'). It's a spectacular Edwardian House and Gardens located about 2 miles from where we live.

How to get there

Mount Ephraim is just off the M2 along a short country lane. If you're using a sat-nav the address to type in is: Hernhill, Faversham, Kent, ME13 9TX

Google Map

Ceremony

Our Wedding Day is on Saturday 4th August 2012. The ceremony starts at 2pm and will take place outside (weather permitting) on the bottom lawn near the pond - we'll put some signs up so you know which way to go.

Dresscode

We want the whole day to be relaxed, so you don't have to come dressed to the nines. If the weather's nice we'll be spending most of the afternoon outside, so wear something cool and comfortable. Shorts and t-shirt are perfectly acceptable, as long as you have nice legs!

Celebrations

After the ceremony, guests will have the opportunity to fill their tummies with some nibbles before we fire up the BBQ at about 6 o'clock. Evening celebrations will kick-off at 8pm with an eclectic mix of music and plenty of alcholic beverages.
